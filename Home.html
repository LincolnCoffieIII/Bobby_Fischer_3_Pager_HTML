Just a little background on Fisher before we jump in!

Bobby Fischer began playing chess at the age of six, and at the age of 15, he attained the title of youngest international grandmaster. He allegedly possessed an I.Q. of 181. He defeated Boris Spassky in 1972, becoming the first world chess champion to be born in the United States. a brilliant eccentric person with a high I.Q. Fischer, who was 181, rose to prominence for his contentious public comments in his final years. He was given Icelandic citizenship in 2005 as a result of legal issues with the US.
